Privacy Policy – HoneyFly

Last updated: February 27, 2026

HoneyFly (“the App”) is a casual mobile game developed and published by an individual developer (“I”, “me”, or “my”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and shares information when you use it.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information I Collect

The App does not ask you to create an account and does not knowingly collect information that directly identifies you (such as your name, address, or phone number).

However, when you use the App, certain information may be automatically collected by me and by third-party service providers (such as advertising and analytics partners), including:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, language, country/region, time zone.
  • Usage data: gameplay events, screens visited, performance data, and crash logs.
  • Advertising identifiers: such as Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA).
  • IP address and coarse location: country or region only, not precise GPS.

This information is typically processed in a pseudonymous or aggregated form for analytics and advertising.

2. How the Information Is Used

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • To analyze performance and player interaction.
  • To display advertisements, including personalized ads (where permitted).
  • To prevent fraud or technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect and process data under their own privacy policies, including:

  • Advertising networks (e.g., Google AdMob).
  • Analytics and crash reporting tools.

These services may use SDKs, cookies, and similar technologies to collect data for measurement and advertising purposes.

4. Advertising and Tracking

The App displays ads, including rewarded ads that provide in-game benefits.

On iOS, the App follows Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may manage tracking permissions in:

Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they may be less personalized.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under the minimum age required by law. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

7. Your Choices

  • You may limit or reset your advertising identifier in device settings.
  • You may change tracking permissions (ATT on iOS).
  • You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the App.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Information may be processed on servers outside your country of residence in accordance with applicable laws.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact options provided on the App Store or relevant marketplace page for HoneyFly.
